# 3D Repair Feedback

Read only in strict reproduction when turning an audit into the next repair.

## Failure Classes

- **Register:** camera, box aspect, scale, crop, subject occupancy, or endpoints
  moved away from L1. Restore final-export registration before local detail.
- **Topology:** chart class, panels, basins, folds, ridges, bars, paths, clusters,
  or separate roles collapsed or gained unsupported supports/walls. Repair the
  visible skeleton before material polish.
- **Footprint:** projected envelope, depth, z-height, density, or role spacing
  shifted. Move the whole record, not one decorative feature.
- **Style:** mesh, facets, contours, strokes, alpha, roughness, marker density,
  bar spacing, or path draw order drifted while topology/register are acceptable.
- **Export:** titles, ticks, labels, z-labels, legends, colorbars, annotations,
  or margins collide or clip. Repair layout with geometry fixed.

## Delta Use

`wrong_direction` switches direction or construction family. `too_small` and
`no_visible_delta` require a visible same-lock bracket. `overshoot` brackets
smaller from control. `right_direction_with_regression` keeps only the approved
cue and rebuilds under locks. `accepted` becomes a preserve item.

Every preserve item is a veto. Feedback names one active dimension, 2-4 visible
landmarks, intended movement, counter-locks, and reject-if tests.
